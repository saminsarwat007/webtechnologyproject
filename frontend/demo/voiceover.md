## Recording tips

- **Pace:** ~2.5 words/sec. If you finish a block early, pause — don't rush into the next line.
- **Practice once** reading the "full script" above while watching the video, then record.
- Tools: **QuickTime** (Mac) or **Audacity** for audio; **iMovie** / **CapCut** to combine with the screen recording.
- **Face cam:** assignment requires your face visible — add a small picture-in-picture overlay in a corner for the whole video, not just the intro.
